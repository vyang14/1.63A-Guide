Ash Crimson Combo Guide

The King of Fighters XV (ver. 1.63)

2-Meter combos

Midscreen

This combo works anywhere on the screen. Delay the first [4]6D a small amount because if too high, the [4]6BD will go under. If this combo reaches the corner, you can add a 4D before the [2]8D at the end.


Corner

Instead of doing 4D 6D [2]8B, you can do 4D [2]8D for 4 less damage.

Note: This combo will drop at the first [4]6D vs Meitenkun. Against him, omit the [4]6A.



Corner Alternative

A bit more difficult as you have to let Ash run farther, but is universal.

4-Meter combos


Midscreen - Universal

This combo works universally, has moderate corner carry and allows Ash to stun from 2A2B (or 2B2A). This Sans Culotte route allows Ash to get the 3rd hit on the second [4]6C post-Sans Culotte, getting you 10 more stun.


Corner

Note: This combo will drop at the first [4]6D vs Meitenkun. Against him, omit the [4]6A AND to get the stun, you will also have to adjust the ender to [2]8D instead of 2D.

5-Meter combos


Fullscreen

This Sans Culotte route will drop vs Heidern, Yamazaki, and Meitenkun.

Note: For Heidern and Yamazaki, change the first [4]6C to [4]6A. This will cause the second [4]6C to get its 3rd hit and give you the same amount of stun.


Almost Fullscreen

This Sans Culotte route is (almost) universal but has significantly less corner carry. The 4D QUICK MAX is auto-timed with this route.

Note: This combo is not automatically timed against Chris/O.Chris and Meitenkun. I recommend using the route in the Fullscreen route in the 5-meter section below.

Character-Specific combos


Meitenkun Corner Carry

This route requires at least 100 stun before QUICK MAX in order to stun for 4 meters. (2B2A2B > 4B or 5C/5D > 4B)


Antonov and Heidern only

This route allows Ash to land a 4th [4]6C on Antonov and Heidern, reducing the need for more hits pre-QUICK MAX activation.
